Time is measured in different units such as seconds, minutes, hours, days, and years. The 24-hour clock is a way to tell time without using "AM" or "PM". After 12:00 noon, the hours continue counting up to 24.
Understanding Time Units
Seconds: The smallest unit of time, 60 seconds = 1 minute.
Minutes: 60 minutes = 1 hour.
Hours: 24 hours = 1 day.
Days, Weeks, Months, Years:
  • 7 days = 1 week.
  • About 30 days = 1 month.
  • 12 months = 1 year.

Understanding the 24-Hour Clock
The 24-hour clock runs from 0:00 (midnight) to 23:59 (one minute before the next midnight). For example, 13:00 is 1 PM, and 17:00 is 5 PM.
