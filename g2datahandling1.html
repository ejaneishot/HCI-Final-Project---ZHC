Collecting and Recording Data
Data is information we collect to answer a question, like "What’s everyone’s favorite color?" or "How many apples do we have?" We can write it down in lists, put it in tables, or draw it using graphs and pictures.
Ways to Show Data:
Lists: A list is just writing the data one by one.

Example:
Favourite fruits:
- Apple
- Banana
- Orange
Ways to Show Data:
Tables: A table organizes the data into rows and columns to make it easy to read.

Example:
Fruit Quantity
Apple 4
Banana 6
Orange 3
