Solve equations with symbols for unknowns

What is solving equations with symbols for unknowns?
Sometimes we don’t know a number in a math problem, so we use a symbol like a box, a triangle, or a letter to stand for the missing number. Our job is to figure out what the missing number is!
How to solve these equations:
  • Look at the equation: for example x + 3 = 7
  • Think about what’s missing: what number plus 3 equals 7? Let’s count up from 3 to 7: 3, 4, 5, 6, 7. That’s 4!
  • Write the answer: So, x = 4, and 4 + 3 = 7.
