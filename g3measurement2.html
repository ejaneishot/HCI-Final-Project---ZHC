Understanding Time Units

Let's understand time units!
Time units help us measure how long something takes or when something happens.
Seconds
Seconds are very short. It takes about 1 second to clap your hands once.

Example:
  • Clapping your hands takes about 1 second.
Minutes
A minute is longer than a second. There are 60 seconds in 1 minute.

Example:
  • Brushing your teeth takes about 30 seconds.
Hours
An hour is longer than a minute. There are 60 minutes in 1 hour.

Example:
  • Watching a movie takes about 1 hour.
Days

Weeks
A week has 7 days.

Example:
  • Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday... up to Sunday make 1 week.
Months
A month has about 30 days, but some months like February are shorter.

Example:
  • Your birthday happens in a specific month.
Years
A year has 12 months or 365 days.

Example:
  • Your next birthday will happen 1 year after your last one.
