Understanding Decimals and Rounding

What are Decimals?
Decimals are numbers that show parts of a whole. They come after a decimal point (.)
Understanding Tenths and Hundredths
Tenths: The first digit after the decimal point is the tenths place. For example:
  • 0.3 means 3 tenths.
  • 0.7 means 7 tenths.
Hundredths: The second digit after the decimal point is the hundredths place. For example:
  • 0.45 means 45 hundredths.
  • 0.99 means 99 hundredths.
