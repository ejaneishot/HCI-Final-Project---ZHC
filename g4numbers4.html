What Are Odd Numbers?
Odd Numbers:
Odd numbers leave 1 leftover when divided by 2.
Examples: 1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 11 and so on!

What Are Even Numbers?
Even Numbers:
Even numbers can be divided by 2 without leftovers.
Examples: 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 12 and so on!

What Are Negative Numbers?
Negative numbers are less than 0. They are written with a minus sign (-) in front of them.
Examples: -1, -5, -10.

Fill in the blank: What is -3 + 5?

Real Life Example of Negative Numbers
Negative numbers are used to measure temperature below 0 degrees.
For example, when 5ºC gets 10ºC colder, it means 5ºC - 10ºC which gives -5ºC.
