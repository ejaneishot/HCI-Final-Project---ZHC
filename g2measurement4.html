Ordering Days and Months

Ordering Days of the Week
There are 7 days in a week, and they always come in the same order.

Days of the Week: Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday.
Examples for Days of the Week
Example 1: If today is Monday, tomorrow will be Tuesday.

Example 2: Saturday and Sunday are the weekend.

Ordering Months of the Year
There are 12 months in a year, and they always come in this order.

Months of the Year: January, February, March, April, May, June, July, August, September, October, November, December.
Examples for Months of the Year
Example 1: Your birthday happens in one month, like May or November.

Example 2: December is the last month of the year, and January is the first.
