Units like meters (m), grams (g), and liters (L) are used to measure different things. We can convert between larger and smaller units, and order them based on size.
Measuring Units
Units like meters (m), grams (g), and liters (L) are used to measure:
  • Length: Meters (m), centimeters (cm), millimeters (mm)
  • Mass: Kilograms (kg), grams (g)
  • Capacity: Liters (L), milliliters (mL)
Examples:
  • A pencil is 15 cm long.
  • A bottle of water is 1.5 L.
  • A book weighs 300 g.

Converting Units
To convert between larger and smaller units:
  • Larger to smaller units:
    • 1 kilogram (kg) = 1000 grams (g)
    • 1 meter (m) = 100 centimeters (cm)
    • 1 liter (L) = 1000 milliliters (mL)
    Example:
    • 2.6 kg = 2600 g
    • 3 meters = 300 cm
  • Smaller to larger units:
    • 1000 grams = 1 kilogram
    • 100 centimeters = 1 meter
    • 1000 milliliters = 1 liter
